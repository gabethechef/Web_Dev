Basic Military Training (BMT)

The Ultimate Guide to BMT

Welcome Recruits! This guide will detail everything you need to know to get through your BMT course. Through this course you will be taught the basics of soidlering and you will get familiar with the countless SAF jargon. The BMT journey is as physcial demanding as it is mental, but fear not for the entire course is progressive.
